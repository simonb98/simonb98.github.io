Story

Hi there, my name is Simon. I am 23 years old and here's my story.
I was born and raised in Aalst (Belgium) and started going to the Chiro (a jouth movement) at the age of 6 where I stayed for 16 years.
12 years as a member and 4 years as a leader. The Chiro definitely shaped the way I am today, an enthousiast, spontanious creative.
Nowadays I am a proud co-chair of the fantastic youth cafe "Jeugdhuis Ypsilon" and part of a team that organizes one of the biggest youth events in Aalst "Nacht van de Jeugdbeweging".

I have always had an interest in the newest tech and wanted to create things with it.
I out my creativity in small apps, websites, visuals like posters, banners, videos, etc.
My friends and family often call me Mr. Do-it-all because I like to do a wild variety of things.
Buying, selling, cleaning sneakers, fixing broken hardware, creating things, organizing events, playing music at parties as a DJ.

As a developer I love designing clean and pretty userfriendly UI, so I would say that I like front-end more than back-end work.
But in the end a good developer has to be able to do both and that is what I'm trying to accomplish, building and creating solid apps with a beautiful design.
